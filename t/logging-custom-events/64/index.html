nico

Hi Community,

Question regarding “Logging Custom Events”, this article How to Log a Custom Event Error has been super useful -thanks @Chandra_Bergmann-. But I wonder what happens to Custom Events that are not ERROR level but INFO or WARNING? Do these get logged somewhere?

Thanks

Chandra_Bergmann

Hi, Nico,

Excellent question! Custom Events, including INFO and WARNING Events, are logged like any other Event. The easiest way to see logged Custom Events (as well as most of the other raw data associated with your apps) while working within the browser-based Airkit Platform is to access the Activity tab in the Console.
