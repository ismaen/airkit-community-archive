Rename an Airkit app on the fly

juanchax

Did you know that you can rename your Airkit app while working on it in Studio?

Ohh yeah! Just double-click on the app name in the title bar, and it will automagically turn into the Rename Application input.
You can then go ahead and input the app’s new name, hit the ‘Enter’ key and … voilà, app successfully renamed.

Renaming an app within Airkit Studio

Keep in mind that this does not create a copy of the application with the new name, it only renames the current app.
