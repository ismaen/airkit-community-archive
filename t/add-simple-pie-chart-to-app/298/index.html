Hello! The easiest way to accomplish this is with https://www.image-charts.com/

Here’s a pie chart similar to your image:

You could make this with Airscript like this:

LET data = JOIN(["90", "10"], ",") IN
URL_ENCODE(
  "https://image-charts.com/chart?chco=3577C6,93C6F1&chd=t:{{data}}&chs=300x400&cht=p3"
)

You could add an “Image” control and set the “source” to the Airscript above.

You can explore the different options for a Pie Chart here: https://editor.image-charts.com/
