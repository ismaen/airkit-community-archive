Hey @Etu !!

Well, there’s a couple of options for this:

  1. When publishing the app from Studio (publish dialog)
  2. Edit the currently deployed app Portal (App/Deployments)

Let’s walk through each one.

Naming your Portal upon publishing your Airkit App

  1. When you click the Publish button in Airkit Studio, the Publish dialog is shown

    publish1646×846 37.2 KB

  2. Go ahead and expand the Advanced section, where the Show Portals section (amongst other options) will be shown

    publish-advanced1646×1576 143 KB

  3. Edit the name to be displayed when accessing the Portal from the Portals’ hamburger menu and hit the Deploy.

Voilá, all done.

Naming/renaming your Portal for a currently deployed app (w/Portals)

  1. After logging in to Airkit, select the app from the app list view

  2. Click on Deployments to access the app’s deployments

    app-list3356×1898 143 KB

  3. Edit the name to be displayed when accessing the Portal from the Portals’ hamburger menu and hit the Deploy

    Portal name3354×1896 298 KB
