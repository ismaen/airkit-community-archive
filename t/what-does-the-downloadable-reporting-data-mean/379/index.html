What does the downloadable reporting data mean?

parzival129

Every app shows an option to download reporting data. Does this reporting data list every session that takes place on the app, and therefore does it also tell me how many sessions I have used in the last month from my plan?

Chandra_Bergmann

Hello, @parzival129! Can you clarify where you’re finding this option?

parzival129

In the apps section of the Airkit Dashboard, if you click on an app, then you will see the “Download Reporting Data” option appear in the popup menu to the right. I believe the app needs to have had active sessions in the past for this data to be available. Apologies for the late reply.

Chandra_Bergmann

Yeah, the app needs to have active Sessions, and the reporting data will list every session that takes place in the app. In a round-about way, this can be used to calculate how many sessions yo have used in the last month from your plan, but you’ll have to do the math yourself.
