We are excited to have you here! This is a space for all and any questions about Airkit and is moderated by Airkit’s Developer Advocacy Team.

What should I do first?

First thing you should do is review our Airkit Community Code of Conduct. Then, get started browsing for answers or asking questions!

This community is meant to bring builders together with Airkit experts so we can all share our knowledge and help each other out.

Where should I go if I can’t find an answer to my question?

First place you can look is in our Documentation or on our Youtube Channel.

If you can’t find an answer there, post a question in the appropriate category, and the community can help you from here!

Join our Slack!

Want to chat with peers and community experts or have a real time discussion regarding Airkit?

Join here!

When should I use the Slack Community?

Our content on this community lasts forever while our Slack has a retention period of 2 weeks. This means that if you have a question that will benefit the community, we’d recommend you asking that in the community.

Happy building!
