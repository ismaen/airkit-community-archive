Right now, Airkit has multiple means of sending emails (either as Notifications or in Data Flows), but there’s no way for Airkit apps to parse incoming emails.

The idea is that in a lot of cases where you could conceivably want to automate full email conversations, a web app would probably work better and have a faster turn around time - and if someone has access to email, they can access a web app.
